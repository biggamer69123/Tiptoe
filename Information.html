Search Engines

A search engine is a software system that provides hyperlinks to web pages and other relevant information on the Web in response to a user's query. The user inputs a query within a web browser or a mobile app, and the search results are often a list of hyperlinks, accompanied by textual summaries and images. Users also have the option of limiting the search to a specific type of results, such as images, videos, or news.

Search Guide

Image Search

Being descriptive helps so try adding details like colors, shapes, objects or other features your image might include.

Use important or specific keywords like "Vibrate Sunset", "Sunset on the Beach"

Combine keywords like adding the word "And"

Exclude unwated terms like "Not" if you keep getting unwanted results

Use Google Images in order to search the image and get similar results.

Video Search

Using quotes in order to search - especially if you're looking for an exact phrase.

You can also eplore related searches which shows up below the search bar to see if you can find anything you're looking for

Filtering helps since you can find the uration or source by using filters like "Source" and "Duration"

Not just that, but you can also adjust the upload data of the video to add an exact date or choose other options

Executing a search

Boolean Operators such as "And", "or" and "not" will narrow down the results.

Wildcard (*) can be used for unknown words to represent a word or phrase you don't know.

You can limit your search to a specific website or domain by using the "site:" operator. Example:machine learning site:wikipedia.org

Add a "filetype:" at the end of your search to get a specific file type. Example presentation example filetype:ppt

Why is it useful?

1. Internet growth & convince

The exponential growth of the Internet has revolutionized human connectivity, reshaping the way we communicate, work, and access information. With over half the global population now online, its pervasive influence is undeniable. From fostering global collaboration to powering e-commerce, the Internet's expansion has become synonymous with progress, offering boundless opportunities for innovation and empowerment.

2. Real-Time Updates

The internet's ability to provide real-time updating data and information has transformed how we access knowledge and stay informed. With instant updates on news, weather, stocks, and more, users can make informed decisions and stay ahead in an ever-changing world. This dynamic flow of information empowers individuals and businesses alike, fostering rapid adaptation and progress in various spheres of life.

3. User Experience

The internet's evolution has prioritized user-friendliness, making it increasingly intuitive and accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds. With streamlined interfaces, intuitive navigation, and user-friendly design, tasks such as browsing, shopping, and communicating have become effortless. This emphasis on ease-of-use has empowered millions worldwide to harness the internet's vast resources, enriching lives and facilitating seamless interaction in the digital age.
