About Us

Our Aim

Help civilians of Myanmar to use ICT to its fullness by creating infromational websites that include turtorials to get them started. These turtorials include cinematic examples, as well as educational videos that show off the applicational features of popular apps.

Our Vision

People these days aren't using technology to its full extent. We think that we need to inform and educate people about how useful it can really be in our daily lives. We try to encourage them by creating this website that shows all the things you need to know about the usage of ICT in our daily lives.

Our Impact

We hope after vising our website, you'll realise how you can really use ICT in your daily lives. Not just only for entertainment, but also for important things like Navigation, Transportation and even simple things like searching on Google.
